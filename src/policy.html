We protect your information from others

If a third party requests your personal information, we will refuse to share it unless you give us permission or we are legally required. When we are legally required to share your personal information, we will tell you in advance, unless we are legally forbidden.

We help customers and partners meet their privacy obligations

Many of the customers and partners using Bindle do not have the benefit of a dedicated privacy team, and it is important to us to help them meet their privacy obligations. To do this, we try to build our products and services so they can easily be used in a privacy-friendly way.

Why we process your information

We generally process your information when we need to do so to fulfill a contractual obligation. European law calls these reasons “legitimate interests.” These “legitimate interests” include:

a.preventing risk and fraud

b.answering questions or providing other types of support

c.providing and improving our products and services

We only process personal information for these “legitimate interests” after considering the potential risks to your privacy—for example, by providing clear transparency into our privacy practices, offering you control over your personal information where appropriate, limiting the information we keep, limiting what we do with your information, who we send your information to, etc.
